Privacy Policy

At ABI Welfare Foundation , we value your privacy and we thank you for trusting us . ABI Welfare Foundation is committed to protecting the privacy of the personal information it obtains from donors, customers, stakeholders and agencies. As part of our Terms and conditions, we have safeguards procedure to protect all private information you provided to us such as Your name and address. The information you provide will be use to deliver services and to keep you informed and up to date on the activities of ABI Welfare Foundation including special events, programs, opportunities to volunteer and funding needs. If at any time you wish to be removed from our mailing lists simply reach out to us by via e-mail at abiwelfarefoundation@gmail.com All information gathered is solely owned by ABI Welfare Foundation and will not be sold, shared or rented under any circumstances.


Donor Rights

• ABI Welfare Foundation will made available comprehensive narrative and financial reports of interventions to you within two weeks of project completion. If you donate towards an ongoing project, You can also request a update every three months or as needed.

• You will be informed of the organization’s mission, of the way the organization intends to use donated resources, and of its capacity to use donations effectively for their intended purposes.

• We assure you that your donations will be used for the purposes for which they were given and You will receive appropriate acknowledgment and recognition.

• At ABI Welfare Foundation, be rest assured that information about your donation will be handle with respect and with confidentiality to the extent provided by law. Also, feel free to ask questions when making a donation and you will receive prompt, truthful, and forthright answers.



ABI Welfare Foundation does not and shall not discriminate on the basis of color, religion, race, gender, disability, gender expression, age, national origin, military status, marital status, and sexual orientation, in any of its services or activities.
