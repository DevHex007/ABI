Our Programs

Our mission statement is achieved through the following programs

End Child Hunger Program

According to the United Nations World Food Program, hunger is a devastating reality of millions of children around the world today especially after the Covid-19 Pandemic. About 45 Million young children suffer from severe malnutrition each year across the globe with the highest rates in countries across Africa. ABI Welfare Foundation is on the frontline in the fight against child hunger. Giving children early and regular access to nutritious meals saves lives. To this end, we organize programs to address food insecurity. We conduct weekly outreaches where foods are distributed to children freely and every week we put smiles on the faces of our ABISTARS.

Educational Support Program

Early childhood education helps to build a child’s cognitive, emotional, physical and social proficiency and prepares them for life’s challenges. Education is the best policy. At ABI Welfare Foundation, we believe that every child deserve access to quality education. Failure to give children quality education limits their futures and denies them opportunities to reach their full potentials. We engage in child education through visits to different schools. We give tuition-free scholarships to indigent students and we distribute school materials such as bags, notebooks, textbooks, writing materials, etc. to all children. We also sponsor and facilitate vocational training for interested students.

Healthcare Program

Health is wealth. We help in the prevention, treatment and management of illnesses and in the preservation of the mental and physical wellbeing of our targeted population. We address the health and wellness of children who are 18 years and below in their communities. We provide easy access to medical care and treatments for every child in need of medical intervention. We have been able to reach out to 9,000 children; giving them access to quality education, healthy meals, and invariably a solid foundation.
